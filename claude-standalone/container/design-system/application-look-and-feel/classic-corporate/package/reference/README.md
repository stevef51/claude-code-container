# Style Reference — Phase 1 UI Design

These page designs were used as the **style reference** for this design system package.

- **UI Design Spec:** Application Design
- **Variant:** V1: Classic Corporate
- **Direction:** Clean, professional design with stark white background and your brand blues as primary accents. Deep navy (#052252) for headers and primary actions, medium blues for secondary elements, cyan highlights for interactive states. Corporate healthcare aesthetic with your logo prominently featured.

## Pages

- **Login Page** — Professional login screen with white background and your brand blues. Clean email/password form with #052252 for primary button. Company logo prominently displayed at top. Trustworthy, established healthcare aesthetic with subtle blue accents on input focus states. (included)
- **Dashboard** — Operational EVS dashboard on white background with your blue color hierarchy. #052252 for header/navigation, #083682 for section headers, #0067b8 for primary actions and highlights, #00b2ee for positive indicators and charts. Company logo in header. Clean data tables and metric cards. (included)
- **Scheduling - Task Assignment** — Task scheduling interface with white background and your brand blues for hierarchy. Roster selection with #0067b8 accents, role assignment with #083682 headers, task forms with #00b2ee highlights. Clear visual separation using your blue palette for different scheduling states. (included)
- **User Management** — Staff management interface with white background. User directory with #052252 headers, #0067b8 action buttons, #00b2ee for active/status indicators. Clean administrative layout with your brand colors defining the visual hierarchy throughout. (included)
